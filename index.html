Vendor Registration Overview
+
One (1) 6’ Table: $110 rent fee (1-day show) $220 rent fee (2-day show) • Table cover • Two (2) Chairs • Display Case for rent is optional • WiFi • Electrical Outlets Accessibility within venue Table assignment(s) will be done at least 1 week before event day. Vendors will be notified via IG.
Vendor Rules & Regulations
+
• Registered vendors must be 18 years or older and present valid ID at check-in & show their IG page at the check-in table. • Two (2) Vendor ID per table will be given at check-in. Only those with Vendor ID may enter before/after show hours. No exceptions. • Vendors + staff limited to max 2 per table. If a 3rd Vendor ID is needed, notify Sin City Card Show staff. • Children of vendors will receive separate designated Vendor ID. • Advertisement of any sort is prohibited at the vendor table.
Setup & Breakdown
+
• Table, chairs, & tablecover will be setup prior to vendor arrival. • Vendors are responsible for setting up their own products on the designated setup day and time. • Vendor tables must remain as laid out by the Vegas TCG Exchange team. They cannot be rearranged. • Vendors must be fully set up before the event opens and remain intact until it concludes. • Breakdown is not permitted until the event has officially ended unless prior notification is given. • Vendors are only allowed to setup on assigned tables & designated areas. • Additional tables may not be added on the day of the event. • Vendors may bring one small rack per registered table. No added chairs or tables of any size.
Product Regulations
+
• Vendors may only sell products specified on their registration form. • All items sold must be legal and comply with local, state, and federal laws. • Stolen, counterfeit or unauthorized merchandise is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate expulsion and a ban from future events.
Conduct & Behavior
+
• Vendors are expected to conduct themselves in a professional and courteous manner at all times. • Harassment, discrimination, violence, or any form of inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated. • Vendors are responsible for maintaining a clean and orderly space. Please leave your area free of trash. • Smoking, vaping, alcohol and all drug substances are prohibited. Vendors caught with prohibited items will be escorted off premises immediately. No exceptions.
Insurance & Liability
+
• Vendors are responsible for obtaining liability insurance for their booth/designated space and merchandise. • The event organizers, venue host & event affiliates are not liable for any loss, theft, or damage to vendor property. • Personal recording devices are recommended for each vendor space as a deterrent and as evidence if needed.
Compliance with Event Staff
+
• Vendors must comply with all instructions and requests from event staff. • Failure to adhere to event rules may result in penalties, including removal from the event and/or being banned from future Vegas TCG Exchange events.
Reselling Tables
+
• Registered vendors are NOT PERMITTED to resell vendor tables unless preapproved by Vegas TCG Exchange with written notice. • All vendors must register via the Vendor Registration Form prior to the event, including anyone splitting a table. • Anyone caught reselling a table will be removed from the event and/or banned from future events. • Any changes to vendor information must be reported to the Vegas TCG Exchange team. • Vendor checks will be conducted throughout the show to ensure a fair marketplace for all.
Amendments
+
The event organizers reserve the right to amend these rules as necessary for the safety and enjoyment of all participants.
Agreement to Terms
+
By registering for the event, vendors agree to abide by these rules and regulations. • Participation as a vendor is subject to approval. Vegas TCG Exchange reserves the right to refuse or revoke vendor privileges at any time.
Payment Information
+
Table selection will be confirmed on a first pay, first receive basis. Table reservations are only guaranteed after full payment is received. A receipt will be emailed — check junk box if not received within 10 minutes.
Refund & Cancellation Policy
+
NO REFUNDS, CREDITS, TRANSFERS, NOR DISCOUNTS WILL BE ISSUED FOR CANCELLATIONS, NO SHOWS, OR EARLY AND/OR REMOVAL FROM THE EVENT FOR ANY REASON. • Applications will be reviewed on a first-come, first-served basis. • Vendors may be placed on a waitlist for the current event. • Vendors are welcome to apply for future shows where availability in their category may open.
